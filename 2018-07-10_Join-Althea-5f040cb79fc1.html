Join Althea

Althea is growing and we are seeking qualified people to join our team!

Althea’s software empowers people with the tools necessary to build decentralized, cryptocurrency powered networks. We are an open source company who believes in decentralized systems, so our team is distributed as well. We are looking for individuals that are focus driven, have a proactive work ethic, and are passionate about our mission.

Positions we have currently available are:

  • Rust Developer — Experienced low level developer interested in either learning rust or working with it full time. Networking and linux experience strongly preferred.
  • Front End Developer — Experience in React preferred but not necessary, since we will be using it. Experience in the Ethereum frontend ecosystem also useful. Being able to make some UX decisions will also be helpful.
  • Marketing Director — Develops and implements brand and marketing strategy. Oversees implementation of the marketing strategy — including campaigns, events, digital marketing, and PR.

If this sounds like you, please get in touch, send us an email at hello@altheamesh.com and fill out the online application.

We look forward to hearing from you!
