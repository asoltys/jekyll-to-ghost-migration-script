Announcing the Althea Ambassadors!

We are proud to announce the formation of our new Ambassador program.

Althea empowers individuals and communities to build and maintain their own sustainable, decentralized internet infrastructure. This is a benefit not only to those who need better access to the internet, but to everyone who believes in the freedom of content and choice of internet providers. This important mission is made possible by fostering connections online and in our communities.

We want to give our enthusiastic supporters all the right tools to spread the word of Althea and decentralized internet. The Ambassador program will equip you with all the knowledge you need to understand the underlying Althea tech and the basics of starting a community mesh or internet service provider. We will provide support and education on all aspects of Althea, including the subnet dao and implementation of on-ground networks. We’ll also help you get your hands on some one-of-a-kind Althea swag!

Althea Ambassadors will get unique access to an ambassador only channel and attend regular meetings, which is a great place to get to know other Althea supporters and share knowledge and resources together. Ambassadors can participate in a variety of ways, including making and responding to online content, testing routers and software, organizing in their communities, and other self-directed projects.

We’re currently seeking talented people who will represent our project and communicate our technology and vision to their local communities and online. It’s an exciting way to get involved with Althea at our earliest stage.

If this sounds like you, you are willing to attend regular meetings and contribute to the vision of a decentralized, community based internet, please fill out the form below. After we have reviewed the submission, a member of the Althea team will be in touch.

Click here to complete the online application.

We look forward to hearing from you!
